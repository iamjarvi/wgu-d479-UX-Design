Plan Your Trip

Holiday Alert: Many attractions and restaurants are closed on National Holidays. Please check the calendar before booking.

Transportation

Public Bus: 5am - 11pm Daily (Taniti City)
Taxis: Available in City Center
Rentals: Cars near Airport, Bikes (Helmets required)

Essentials

Power: 120 Volts (US Standard)
Currency: US Dollar (Euros/Yen accepted)
Language: English spoken in tourist areas

Health & Safety

Hospital: 1 Main Hospital, several clinics
Crime: Violent crime is rare. Watch for pickpockets in crowded areas.
Alcohol: Legal age 18. No sales Midnight-9am.
