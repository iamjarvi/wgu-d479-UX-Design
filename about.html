About Taniti

Taniti is a small Pacific island known for white-sand beaches, lush rainforests, and a warm, welcoming culture. The island's economy depends on tourism; this site helps visitors plan short stopovers and longer stays with curated activities and clear logistics.

Geography

White-sand bays, volcanic interior, and coastal reefs make Taniti ideal for both relaxation and adventure.

Culture

Local artisan markets, traditional music, and island cuisine are part of the visitor experience.
